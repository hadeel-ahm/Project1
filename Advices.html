Tips to overcome boredom

1-Structure your day

Having a structure or schedule can help you feel more present and way too focusedsed. You can create a new routine to be followed by incorporating time for work, daily chores, leisure, social interactions, and most importantly, for me time

2-START A NEW RITUAL

Find a suitable activity that will help you cope with the current scenario and make it a solid daily ritual to help yourself stay calm. This can include maintaining a journal or even something as simple as having a cup full of tea in the morning.

3-CHANGE YOUR PERCEPTION

It is helpful to change the way you think about your isolation or your boredom. Think of this given alone-time as an opportunity for you to learn more about yourself and pick up a new skill; if you want!

4-RECONNECT WITH YOUR FRIENDS

Reach out and talk to old friends via phone, messages, social media, or video calls. This can help you feel well-connected even though you might not be in their company always
